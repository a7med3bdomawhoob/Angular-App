Integrating AI Technology and GIS for Advanced Road and Infrastructure Management As part of its ongoing commitment to innovation in asset and infrastructure management, Namaa is proud to announce the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology to detect cracks and distresses in paved roads. This significant advancement allows for precise and efficient assessment of road conditions, enabling better identification of maintenance needs and improving long-term road performance. In addition, Namaa has integrated AI with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to detect road assets such as signs, barriers, and light poles. This combination allows for continuous monitoring and updating of these assets, facilitating data-driven decision-making and enhancing road maintenance and management. These developments mark a major leap in Namaa’s ability to provide advanced, intelligent solutions for improving infrastructure quality and increasing project efficiency to better serve cities and communities.
