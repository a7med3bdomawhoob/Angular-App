About Namaa

Founded in 1998, Namaa Company for Engineering Consultations was established with a vision to contribute significantly to the urbanization and construction development of Egypt and the Arab world. Under the esteemed leadership of Dr. Essam Sharaf, who has been our Chairman since inception, Namaa has grown to become a trusted name in engineering and consulting. With a professional task force of over 400 experts and several offices across Egypt and Saudi Arabia, we have adopted a methodology of sustainable management to deliver superior and timely services. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our optimal application of consulting services, addressing the most pressing needs in the market.

Our Values

We put the well-being and fulfillment of our team members and the success of our clients at the utmost priority.
